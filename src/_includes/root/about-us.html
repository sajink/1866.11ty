Step Into The Home of Rich History, Timeless Elegance & Warm Hospitality

Welcome to Heritage 1866. Nestled in the picturesque Melur village near Dharmadam in Thalassery, this 158-year-old heritage home, once the proud residence of an Indian freedom fighter, stands as a testament to unwavering spirit and cultural richness. Immerse yourself in the vintage opulence of traditional Kerala architecture, elegant antiques, and cherished artefacts which whisper stories of a bygone era.

Unwind yourself in spacious, elegantly appointed rooms, each a haven of tranquillity. Indulge in authentic Thalassery cuisine, prepared with the freshest local produce.

More than just a stay, we offer a curated journey into the vibrant heart of this storied spice port. Beyond your tranquil haven, explore the soul of Thalassery. Immerse yourself in the electrifying energy of Theyyam performances, where ancient rituals come alive. Savour the magic of Thalassery Biryani, a legendary local delicacy. Wander through bustling markets overflowing with the freshest spices, their enticing aromas transporting you back to Thalassery's trading heyday.

Step into Heritage 1866, and immerse yourself in the fine blend of rich history, timeless elegance and warm hospitality.
