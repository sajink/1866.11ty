Authentic Thalassery Cuisine

Savour an exquisite journey through taste and time set in a vintage ambiance.

Indulge in the unique flavours of Thalassery, lovingly crafted by local cooks with time-honoured recipes.

Thalassery's culinary scene shines with its fragrant biryani,cooked with love and bursting with spices - a legacy of Arab influence. But it's true charm lies in the diverse snacks. Sweet treats like muttamala (egg yolk noodles) and unnakaya (fried bananas) offer a delightful contrast to savoury bites like ari pathiri (spiced flatbreads) and kallummakaya (mussel) specialties. Each bite, a unique blend of local and borrowed traditions, making Thalassery a foodie's paradise.
